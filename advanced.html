Half Lotus Crow

The Half Lotus Crow is an advanced yoga arm balance pose that combines the Lotus leg position with the Crow pose. It requires strength, balance, and flexibility. Refer the video and follow these steps:

  1. Begin in a squat position.
  2. Place your right ankle on your left thigh (half Lotus).
  3. Plant your palms on the mat, shoulder-width apart.
  4. Lean forward, shifting your weight onto your hands.
  5. Bend your elbows and bring your knees towards your right elbow.
  6. Lift your feet off the mat one at a time.
  7. Balance in the Half Lotus Crow pose for a few breaths.
One-Legged Wheel

The One-Legged Wheel Pose, or Eka Pada Urdhva Dhanurasana, is an advanced yoga backbend that requires flexibility, strength, and balance. Refer the video and follow these steps:

  1. Start in a Bridge Pose with hands and feet pressing into the mat.
  2. Lift your right foot off the ground.
  3. Extend your right leg straight up while balancing on your left foot and hands.
  4. Hold for a few breaths.
  5. Gently lower your right leg and return to the mat.
  6. Switch to the other side, lifting your left leg.
Standing Flying Crow

The Standing Flying Crow is a challenging and dynamic yoga pose that combines a standing split with an arm balance. Refer the video and follow these steps:

  1. Start standing with feet hip-width apart.
  2. Lift your right leg into a standing split.
  3. Place your hands on the mat beside your left foot.
  4. Shift weight forward, balancing on your hands, and bring knees to your upper arms (Crow Pose).
  5. Hold for a few breaths, then return to a standing split.
  6. Repeat on the other side with your left leg.
Forearm Stand

.The Forearm Stand, also known as Pincha Mayurasana, is an advanced yoga inversion that involves balancing on your forearms with your legs extended upward. Refer the video and follow these steps:

  1. Start lying on your back with legs extended and arms overhead.
  2. Inhale, engage your core, and exhale as you roll up, reaching for your toes.
  3. Inhale at the top, sitting tall and folding forward.
  4. Exhale as you roll back down one vertebra at a time, keeping legs straight.
  5. Repeat 6-8 times, focusing on control and core engagement
Splits Pose

The Splits Pose, Hanumanasana, is an advanced yoga pose that focuses on flexibility in the hips and hamstrings. Refer the video and follow these steps:

  1. Start kneeling on your mat.
  2. Extend your right leg forward and slide your left leg back.
  3. Keep your hips squared and aligned.
  4. Engage your core for support.
  5. To go deeper, reach your chest forward.
  6. Hold for several breaths, then release and switch sides.
