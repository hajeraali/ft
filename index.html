HIIT ・❥・

High-intensity interval training (HIIT) is a method of exercising that involves short, fast bursts of exercise interspersed with periods of rest. It has been praised for its numerous health benefits and it can help you to get in shape quickly.
1. It improves your stamina
2. It increases your strength
3. You’ll burns lots of fat
4. It keeps your heart healthy
5. It can help regulate your blood sugar levels
6. It can help to strengthen your bones
7.Eases muscle stiffness

WEIGHT LIFTING ・❥・

Strength training is also known as weight training, resistance training, and muscular training. The general definition of strength training is any physical movement in which you use your body weight or equipment to build muscle mass, strength, and endurance . Depending on the type of strength training you choose to reach your goals, you can use various equipment (or none at all), such as
1.Body weight: using your own body weight and the force of gravity to perform various movements (e.g., pushups, squats, planks, pullups, and lunges)
2.Free weights: equipment not bound to the floor or a machine, such as dumbbells, barbells, kettlebells, medicine balls, or objects around the house
3.Resistance bands/loop bands: rubber bands that provide resistance when stretched
4. Weight machines: machines with adjustable weights or hydraulics attached to provide resistance and stress to the muscles





YOGA AND PILATES ・❥・

Both Pilates and yoga are low-impact exercises, but there is one important difference. When practicing yoga, you typically adopt a position and hold it, or flow into a different position. In Pilates, you adopt a position and then challenge your core by moving your arms or legs. Both improve increase strength and flexibility.
Yoga is an ancient spiritual practice rooted in India. As a fitness strategy, it blends physical poses (asanas) with breathing techniques(pranayama). Because it has some elements of mindfulness, yoga is sometimes called meditative movement.
Benefits include:
1. Increase core strength to improve stability
2. Improve posture
3. Increase flexibility
4. Ease lower back painy
5. studies have shown that Pilates may help with weight control.
