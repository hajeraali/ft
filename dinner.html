Grilled Lemon Herb Chicken
with Quinoa and Roasted Vegetables

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 lemons, juiced and zested
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh thyme, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Mixed vegetables (such as bell peppers, zucchini, and cherry tomatoes)

Instructions:

  1. Chicken:In a bowl, mix the lemon juice, lemon zest, minced garlic, chopped rosemary, thyme, parsley, salt, pepper, and olive oil to create the marinade. Place the chicken breasts in a resealable plastic bag or a shallow dish, and pour the marinade over them. Seal the bag or cover the dish and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to marinate. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Remove the chicken from the marinade and grill for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until the chicken is cooked through and has grill marks.

  2. Quinoa:. Rinse the 1 cup quinoa under cold water to remove any bitterness. In a medium saucepan, combine the quinoa and water (or chicken broth) and add a pinch of salt. Bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the quinoa is cooked and the liquid is absorbed. Fluff with a fork.

  3. Roasted Vegetables Take 2 cups of mixed vegetables (such as bell peppers, zucchini, and cherry tomatoes) Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Toss the mixed vegetables with olive oil, salt, pepper, and dried Italian seasoning (if using). Spread the seasoned vegetables on a baking sheet in a single layer. Roast in the preheated oven for about 15-20 minutes, or until they are tender and slightly caramelized.

  4. To Serve: Plate the grilled lemon herb chicken alongside a serving of quinoa and roasted vegetables. You can garnish the dish with additional fresh herbs or a drizzle of lemon juice for extra flavor.
